It all started with too many drinks!

Friends of mine and I went drinking. At some point, I cannot fully recall why (and why this happened on the loo), I was negotiating about one of my friends' soul. But I remember that I was a terrific negotiator and was able to purchase his soul for ridiculously cheap 2â‚¬. And there is proof! After returning from the loo, he wrote the devilish contract on a napkin:

Sorry, about the low quality, probably resulting from our intoxication. It's in German, but it translates to:

Hereby do I, censored name of my friend, in full command of my mental faculties [not so sure about that!] and of my own free will, sell my "soul" to Robert Meyer [that's me] for 2Euro.

However, irresponsible me already managed to lose the napkin during that night! Soo sad! Only if there was a tamper-proof method to preserve any contract forever... As it turns out, there is: The glorious, holy Blockchain! A couple of days later this idea was born!

Accordingly, I came up with this new SOUL Ethereum token. With it you can sell your soul to total strangers and make some money! But beware of the consequences:

In order to use this website in it's full bloom and in order to buy and sell souls, you need one of the best Chrome plugins ever made: MetaMask. It connects you to the Ethereum Blockchain in your browser and let's you carry out transactions!

If you buy someone's soul, you will also be rewarded with tokens, called Soul Napkins! Per Ether spent on people's souls, you get 1000 napkins!

Lastly, selling your soul and buying souls is, of course, not for free! Charon, who helps me with this nifty business, charges you for advertising and soul transfers. Still, to not look to greedy, half of the profits made with this website will be donated to the Giordana Bruno Foundation. The foundation supports evolutionary Humanism and Secularism. The other half of the profits will most likely be spent on (more) drinks, though!

Sell Your Soul!

You can sell your soul to total strangers! Charon charges you a small fee of 13 finney for advertising your soul in his soul book. Moreover, in case your soul is bought by someone, Charon further deducts a 10% transfer fee (much like the. For example, if you want to sell your soul for 1 Ether, you have to pay 13 finney upfront. In case someone decides to buy your soul, you will receive 0.9 Ether and 0.1 are kept by Charon!

To sell your soul just enter your price below and add some small comment (whatever comes to your mind) and hit the button! All of this will be stored forever and irreversibly on the Ethereum Blockchain! So I hope you thought it through and have chosen your words wisely!
